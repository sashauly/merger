# Слияние / Merger 📼

**Merger** is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that recreates the distinct, tactile workflow and creative limitations of vintage 4-track cassette multitrack recorders right in your browser. 

The name is inspired by the historic confluence of the Moskva and Oka rivers in Kolomna, serving as a direct metaphor for the art of mixing: summing four independent audio channels into a unified stereo master.

## 🎛 Workflow & Limitations

This application focuses on the strict constraints of classic tabletop recording setups, forcing deliberate mixing choices and tactical track bounces:

*   **Fixed 4-Track Layout:** Four dedicated, parallel channels with dedicated leveling and spatial routing controls.
*   **Dual-Band Sculpting:** Fixed high and low shelf adjustments on every channel strip to shape your sound.
*   **Varispeed Simulation:** Integrated speed adjustments that shift pitch and playback rate simultaneously, reproducing vintage hardware behavior.
*   **Multitrack Bouncing:** Merge and bounce multiple captured tracks onto a single channel to free up space for overdubbing.
*   **Tactile Aesthetic:** A utilitarian interface styled after industrial design principles, prioritizing visual feedback and physical knobs.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Open the application in any modern browser, load or record your stems, and start blending your tracks. Since this is a PWA, it can be installed on your desktop or mobile device for completely offline operation.
